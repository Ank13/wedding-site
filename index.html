Planes

The most convienent airport is Chicago O'Hare International Airport (ORD), which hosts most major airlines.

If you are a Southwest Airlines frequent flier, then you might consider Chicago Midway International Airport (MDW), but it is much farther from Highland Park than O'Hare.

American Taxi is a special service to the suburbs of Chicago. Make a reservation or call from the airport when you arrive.

Trains

Chicago's Metra runs between downtown Chicago and Highland Park. Get on at Ogilvie Transportation Center (buy tickets in station to avoid surcharge), and get off at Ravinia (not Ravinia Park). The ride is approximately 45 minutes on a Sunday morning, and one-way ticket costs $5.25. Ravinia station is 0.7 miles from the synagogue. Either walk, or contact us to arrange a ride.
